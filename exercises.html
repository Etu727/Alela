Home Exercises

Push-Ups

- Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps muscles. - Helps build upper body strength and endurance. - Can be done with variations like incline, decline, or diamond push-ups to target different muscle groups.

Squats

- Works the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstring muscles. - Improves lower body strength and stability. - Can be done with bodyweight, holding weights, or using a wall for support.

Lunges

- Targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings. - Improves balance, coordination, and lower body strength. - Can be done with bodyweight or holding weights.

Planks

- Engages the core muscles, including the abs, obliques, and lower back. - Improves posture, stability, and balance. - Can be done in various positions, such as forearm plank, side plank, or reverse plank.

Jumping Jacks

- Provides a full-body cardio workout. - Improves cardiovascular fitness and endurance. - Can be done in intervals or continuously for a more intense workout.

Burpees

- Combines a push-up, a squat, and a jump. - Works the entire body, including the arms, legs, and core. - Increases heart rate and improves overall fitness

Crunches

- Targets the abdominal muscles. - Helps strengthen the core and improve posture. - Can be done with variations like reverse crunches or bicycle crunches

Leg Raises

- Focuses on the lower abdominal muscles. - Improves core strength and stability. - Can be done lying on the floor or hanging from a pull-up bar.
